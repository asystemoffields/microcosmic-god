# Universal Genesis

Universal Genesis is a speculative side project: a far more realistic simulation that starts from a big-bang-like initial condition, seeds no life, and lets physics create whatever structure it can.

This is not the near-term Microcosmic God simulator. It is a long-horizon north star for thinking about emergence at a much deeper scale.

## Core Thought

Microcosmic God currently starts after many conveniences already exist:

- localized habitats
- typed energy gradients
- materials
- organisms
- reproduction
- neural agents
- tools
- communication channels

Universal Genesis removes those conveniences. The simulation begins with no organisms, no genomes, no tools, and no preselected biosphere. If life appears, it appears because matter, energy, chemistry, and planetary conditions made it possible.

## Why It Matters

This version would test the most extreme version of the project philosophy:

```text
encode laws, not outcomes
```

It would ask whether increasingly complex structure can arise through:

- expansion/cooling
- field interactions
- particle formation
- gravity
- star and planet formation
- thermodynamics
- chemistry
- autocatalysis
- self-maintaining boundaries
- replication
- ecology
- learning
- intelligence

## Why It Is Brutal

A literal simulation from big bang to life is computationally absurd for this project.

Even with radical abstraction and huge speedups, most runs would likely spend enormous time producing nothing recognizably alive. A 400-hour run might mostly show structure formation, sterile chemistry, or dead-end complexity.

That is acceptable for a side project, but it would be a mistake to let this ambition slow the practical artificial-life sandbox.

## Possible Abstraction Ladder

A realistic version would need layers, not one monolithic simulator:

1. Cosmological scaffold:
   - expansion
   - temperature decline
   - density variation
   - gravity wells

2. Astrophysical structure:
   - stars
   - planetary systems
   - radiation gradients
   - heavy-element availability

3. Planetary physics:
   - geology
   - oceans
   - atmosphere
   - weathering
   - geothermal gradients
   - impact events

4. Chemistry:
   - reaction networks
   - catalysis
   - solvents
   - membranes/boundaries
   - energy gradients
   - molecular persistence

5. Proto-life:
   - autocatalytic sets
   - compartmentalization
   - replication with variation
   - metabolism-like cycles
   - heredity-like persistence

6. Biology:
   - cells or cell analogs
   - ecology
   - evolution
   - multicellularity-like aggregation
   - nervous systems or control networks

7. Intelligence:
   - learning
   - communication
   - tool use
   - culture

## The Useful Compromise

The realistic path is probably not full cosmology first. It is a family of staged, unseeded worlds:

- chemistry-only abiogenesis toy worlds
- planet-scale energy-gradient worlds
- ocean vent worlds
- tidal pool worlds
- ice/ocean interface worlds
- dry mineral surface worlds
- comet/impact chemistry worlds

Each stage should seed only physical conditions, not life. If a stage produces persistent replicators or proto-organisms, those can become initial conditions for the next layer.

## Relationship To Main Microcosmic God

The main simulator can borrow principles from Universal Genesis:

- richer physics fields
- material law consistency
- no hidden objectives
- no guaranteed life success
- energy gradients before organisms
- chemistry and tools as continuous extensions of physics

But the main simulator may still seed organisms because its purpose is to study evolved intelligence and artificial life on human-manageable timescales.

## First Prototype Idea

A tiny Universal Genesis prototype could be:

```text
graph of planetary chemical microenvironments
  nodes: vents, pores, pools, mineral surfaces, ice pockets
  fields: heat, pH-like acidity, solvent level, salinity, UV/radiation, redox gradient
  materials: simple molecule classes and catalysts
  rules: diffusion, reaction, decay, catalysis, membrane formation
  success condition for observation only: persistent autocatalytic cycles
```

No reward. No seeded life. Just chemistry and persistence.

If persistent autocatalytic cycles appear, we archive them the same way we archive interesting brains: as rare emergent structures worth studying, not as objectives the simulator was optimizing for.

## Guardrail

Universal Genesis should inspire the physics and emergence philosophy, but it should remain separate until Microcosmic God has a strong, inspectable, scalable core.
